History of Gwili Gwili and the Formation of G2 Unite

Gwili Gwili began as a small grassroots movement rooted in community upliftment and local empowerment. Originating from a desire to create meaningful change in underdeveloped areas, the initiative was born from conversations, shared struggles, and a united vision of building better futures—particularly for young people.

As the need for structured development became more evident, the founding members recognized that action had to go beyond conversation. This led to the birth of G2 Unite—a dynamic organisation formed with the mission to bridge the gap of development across communities.

Since its inception, G2 Unite has launched a variety of tailor-made programmes designed to engage all age groups. These programmes promote education, mentorship, leadership, and community involvement — empowering the youth while connecting with elders to share wisdom and tradition.

G2 Unite Founder

G2 Unite is an organization founded by Mhlawuleli Malusi, established to bridge the gap in community development. Our year-round programs address the diverse needs of all age groups.

A major focus is Youth Development and Empowerment, equipping young people with the skills and support they need. Intergenerational programs also promote mutual learning between elders and youth.
