G2 UNITE PROJECTS


G2 Unite is actively involved in community projects aimed at uplifting the youth, demonstrating a strong commitment to social responsibility and empowerment. By organizing and participating in mentorship programs, educational workshops, and youth development initiatives, G2 Unite creates valuable opportunities for young people to grow, learn, and realize their potential. The organization partners with local schools, nonprofits, and community leaders to ensure these efforts are impactful and inclusive. Through these initiatives, G2 Unite not only fosters leadership and life skills among the youth but also helps build a stronger, more resilient community.



G2 Unite Proudly Sponsors G2 United Rugby Club


At G2 Unite, we are committed to more than just academic development—we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals through sport, leadership, and teamwork. That’s why we are proud to announce our sponsorship of the G2 United Rugby Club. This partnership represents our dedication to uplifting the youth in our communities, not only through education but also through sports development and community engagement. Rugby is more than a game—it’s a platform that teaches discipline, teamwork, perseverance, and pride. These are the same values we uphold at G2 Unite.

Our proactive and innovative approach to our challenges has allowed us to successfully complete these projects as per the project scope.

G2 Unite Start-Up Bursary


G2 Unite Offers Start-Up Bursary to Empower Community Students G2 Unite is proud to support the development of an educated society by offering a Start-Up Bursary aimed at uplifting students from local communities. The initiative seeks to alleviate financial barriers and open doors to higher education, reinforcing the belief that through education, we can transform many of our households.

The bursary is open to students who meet the following minimum criteria: Must provide a valid Acceptance Letter from a Higher Institution. Preference is given to students currently in Grade 12 at Zwelamandlovu SSS. Former students who have been out of school and sitting at home for the past 3 years are also encouraged to apply. Most importantly, applicants must show a genuine desire to pursue their studies and make a change in their lives.

In the words of G2 Unite: “Black child, there is hope!”.
